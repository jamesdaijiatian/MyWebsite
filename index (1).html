How does social media affect people's consumer behavior in China? A quantitative model to reveal the relationships

This paper examines how social media factors influence consumer purchasing behavior in China, using perceived risk and value as mediating variables. Based on 100 survey responses and quantitative analysis via SPSSAU24, the study constructs a theoretical model to explain the relationship between social media and consumer decision-making.
